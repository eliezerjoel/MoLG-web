Home

The Ministry of Local Government (MoLG) in Uganda is a cabinet-level ministry responsible for overseeing and supporting local governments. Its primary mandate is to ensure sustainable, efficient, and effective service delivery through a decentralized governance system. This includes harmonizing local government functions, promoting decentralization, and coordinating implementation of policies at the local level. The ministry also plays a crucial role in mentoring, compliance inspection, and supervision of local governments.

Our Mission

To coordinate and support Local Governments for sustainable, efficient and effective service delivery, within the decentralized system.

Our Vision

To have a democratic, participatory, decentralized Local Government System that can sustain development and deliver services efficiently and effectively to the people.
